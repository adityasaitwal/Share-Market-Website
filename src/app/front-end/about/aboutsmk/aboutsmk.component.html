About SharemarketKranti

Sharemarketkranti is one of the top institutes in Pune.

Established in 2018, SMK (Sharemarketkranti) has successfully trained over more than 5000 students all across India. Our aim is to educate people on all segments of stock market so that they become financially independent. We have successfully changed the fortune of many of our students by increasing their passive income through our most renowned strategies. We provide theoretical & practical training on Equity Market, Commodity Market, Currency Market, Derivatives Market, etc. We specialize in providing personal assistance to our students by sitting with them while they trade in live market. With two offices in Pune & more than 30 staff, we are driving to change the fortune for many interested students all over the country.
